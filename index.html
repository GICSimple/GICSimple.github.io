What does GIC simple offer?

Earn more and access your money faster with our short term and redeemable GICs.

Earn more on your cash, all the time.
Maximize your savings with our constantly updated GIC portfolios, tailored for both short-term and long-term goals. We'll remind you to reinvest at maturity, ensuring your money always earns top rates.
Your deposits are fully insured.
GICs are among Canada's safest investments, insured federally or provincially based on the institution. GIC Simple ensures your savings are optimally allocated across multiple institutions, keeping within insurance limits, without ever handling your cash directly.
Access to over 20 Financial Institution GIC issuers.
Build a high-yielding GIC portfolio with GIC Simple, accessing top rates across Canada from home. Invest easily in your preferred institutions and monitor your portfolio in real time, 24/7. We'll also alert you to upcoming maturities.
Fully digital, simple, secure application.
Invest at your convenience; only you can initiate transactions. Your GIC order is confirmed electronically, eliminating the wait for traditional paper processes. We prioritize your privacy and security, ensuring your data remains protected on our platform.
